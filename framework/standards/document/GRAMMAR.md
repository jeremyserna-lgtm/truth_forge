# GRAMMAR

**Naming follows THE GRAMMAR. Mark, voice, layer.**

---

## The Rule

Grammar is ontology. How something is written tells you what it IS.

---

## The Three Marks

| Mark | Who | Voice | Layer |
|------|-----|-------|-------|
| **:** (colon) | ME | ALL CAPS | Framework |
| **-** (hyphen) | US | Normal Caps | Product |
| **_** (underscore) | NOT-ME | no caps | Technical |

---

## Document Naming

| Layer | Convention | Example |
|-------|------------|---------|
| Theory | `NN_NAME.md` | `00_GENESIS.md` |
| Meta | `STANDARD_NAME.md` | `STANDARD_LIMIT.md` |
| Specifics | `name/INDEX.md` | `document/INDEX.md` |
| Primitives | `NAME.md` | `LENGTH.md` |

---

## Folder Naming

Folders are infrastructure. Infrastructure is NOT-ME.

```
lowercase_underscore/
```

---

## Content Naming

| Context | Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Concepts | `COLON:SEPARATED` | `ME:NOT-ME` |
| Products | `Hyphen-Separated` | `Truth-Forge` |
| Code | `underscore_separated` | `truth_forge/` |

---

## The Descent

```
ME:NOT-ME        ← colon + ALL CAPS
    ↓
Not-Me           ← hyphen + Normal Caps
    ↓
not_me           ← underscore + no caps
```
