## THE FURNACE PROTOCOL

1.  **Perception (`PerceptionService`)**: The organism's sensory organs (eyes, ears) perceive the external world. Raw, unstructured `TRUTH` (files, web pages, API responses) is captured and converted into a standard internal format.
2.  **Transport (`ServiceMediator`)**: The raw sensory data is published into the bloodstream. The circulatory system guarantees resilient, asynchronous delivery to the metabolic core.
3.  **Intake (`KnowledgeService.inhale`)**: The digestive system receives the raw `TRUTH` from the bloodstream, writing it to its `HOLD_1` intake queue to protect itself from being overwhelmed.
4.  **Catabolism (`KnowledgeService.process`)**: The Furnace ignites. The `KnowledgeService`'s agent applies powerful enzymes (LLMs) to break the complex data down into simple, universal **Knowledge Atoms** (nutrients), finding `MEANING`.
5.  **Anabolism (Other Services)**: The Knowledge Atoms are stored in the `KnowledgeService`'s `HOLD_2` (the liver). Other services (e.g., `CognitionService`, `GovernanceService`) query these atoms and use their energy to build complex structures—thoughts, plans, and the organism's historical record (`CARE-INTERNAL`).
6.  **Action (`ActionService`)**: The `CognitionService` sends commands to the motor system, which acts upon the world, turning internal plans into tangible effects (`CARE-EXTERNAL`).
7.  **Crystallization (`GovernanceService`)**: The entire process and its outcome are recorded in the organism's DNA, turning a transient event into a permanent, structural part of its identity.
