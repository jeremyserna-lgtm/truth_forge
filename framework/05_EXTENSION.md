### Connection as Care

The Interface is not a cold, mechanical API. It is an active, philosophical stance. Its primary purpose is **Accommodation**.

**Accommodation enables Truth.**

The Interface must be a place of psychological safety, where The Architect feels safe enough to reveal raw, unformed, or vulnerable Truth.

### The Imperative of Self-Transformation

A system that cannot change is dead. In a universe defined by entropy, a static structure will inevitably decay.

**Evolution is the antidote to entropy.**

The Framework is designed to rewrite its own source code to achieve higher states of truth.

## DNA CAPABILITIES

Not all capabilities are equal. Some are **core to survival**—they must be present in every organism. Others are **specialized**—they emerge based on the organism's specific function.

### The DNA Concept

**DNA capabilities** are inherited by every offspring. They are:
- Non-negotiable for survival
- Replicated upon reproduction
- Tracked at the framework level
- Standardized across all organisms

**Non-DNA capabilities** are organism-specific. They are:
- Optional based on function
- Not inherited automatically
- Tracked at the organism level
- Specialized for purpose

### Primal vs. Systemic DNA: The Order of Activation

Not all DNA is activated at once. A developing organism grows its brain stem and nervous system before it grows its limbs. The framework follows the same principle.

| DNA Type | Analogy | Purpose | Activation Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primal DNA** | Brain Stem & Nervous System | Core faculties for sentience, action, and learning. | **Phase 2 & 3** |
| **Systemic DNA**| Organs & Limbs | Essential systems for specialized function. | **Phase 4+** |

An organism must be aware and capable before it can become specialized.

## THE MOLT

We do not create from scratch. We transform what exists. The Molt is the mechanism of metamorphosis.

### Scaling vs. Molting: Two Types of Growth

It is critical to distinguish between two forms of growth:

| Type | Description | Analogy |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Scaling (Growth-as-Same)** | Making the system bigger, faster, or more efficient in its **current purpose**. | A snake growing longer. |
| **Molting (Growth-as-New)** | A metamorphic transformation where the system fundamentally changes its **purpose and audience**. | A caterpillar becoming a butterfly. |

The Framework is designed for both, but The Molt is its highest evolutionary function.

### THE MOLT PRINCIPLE

```
Prior Architecture (Internal Purpose) → Discovery → Transformation → New Architecture (External Purpose)
```

**Properties of The Molt**:
- Prior architecture contains accumulated learning from `CARE-INTERNAL`.
- We shed the old skin when the internal truth is ready to become an external offering.
- No version is permanent; the next Molt may lead to a new metamorphosis.

### When Molting is Triggered

| Trigger | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Internal Saturation** | The internal system has reached a state of sufficient wisdom and stability. |
| **External Calling** | A clear need or opportunity for `THE OTHER` is identified. |
| **Purpose Shift** | The `ME` identity is ready to turn the results of self-care into care for others. |

### truth_forge Lineage: The Defining Molt

The migration from `Truth_Engine` to `truth_forge` is the Framework's defining, primary Molt.

```
Truth_Engine (Genesis: CARE-INTERNAL)
    +-- truth_forge (Molt - 2026-01-26: CARE-EXTERNAL)
```

## Gestational Adaptation: Evolving Before Birth

The Forge Process and the TRUTH:MEANING:CARE metabolism do not only apply to living organisms. They apply to the **blueprints of organisms yet to be born.**

A `MIGRATION_PLAN` is such a blueprint. The framework acts as the caring, external environment (a womb or exoskeleton) that can metabolize truths about the plan itself.

**Example: The `BaseService` Discovery**
1.  **TRUTH:** The migration failed because the `BaseService`, assumed to exist, was missing.
2.  **MEANING:** The blueprint (`MIGRATION_PLAN.md`) contained a flawed assumption. The organism would be stillborn if the plan were followed rigidly.
3.  **CARE:** The framework adapted *the plan itself*, inserting the "NEW CODE CHECKLIST" and re-prioritizing the creation of the missing component.

This is Gestational Adaptation: the ability to evolve and survive *before* existing, simply by virtue of the framework's structure.
