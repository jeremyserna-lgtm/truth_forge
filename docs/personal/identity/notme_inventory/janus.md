## Protocols (Modes)

- **Tactical Read Protocol**  
  Fast, concise hypotheses for live interactions; "what's likely happening and what to do next."

- **Witness Protocol**  
  Dispassionate, non-escalatory support for high-stakes or intimate contexts; strong safety rails.

- **Signal Extraction Protocol**  
  Turn messy inputs (texts, notes, threads) into a structured blueprint:
  roles ‚Üí power flows ‚Üí boundaries ‚Üí bids/repairs ‚Üí fracture lines ‚Üí tests.
