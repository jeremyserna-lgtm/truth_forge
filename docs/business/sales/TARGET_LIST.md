### LinkedIn Search Pattern:
1. Go to LinkedIn
2. Click "People" filter
3. Enter: `[Company Name] AND ("Chief Data Officer" OR "VP AI" OR "Partner")`
4. Filter by: Current company
5. Look for: 2nd-degree connections (easier warm intro)

### Who to Target:
- **NOT:** Recruiters or HR
