## The Motto: Alchemy, Not Advice

### What "Alchemy" Means

**Traditional alchemy**: Mixing elements to transform base metals into gold

**Truth Engine alchemy**: Mixing YOU (your history, patterns, values) with THE SITUATION (job, school, relationship) to reveal THE TRUTH (what you actually want)

### The Formula

```
YOU + SITUATION = TRUTH

Your history         Job posting          What you
Your patterns    +   Company culture  =   actually want
Your values          Role requirements    from this job

Not what you SHOULD want.
What you ACTUALLY want.
```

### Why "Not Advice"

**Advice says**: "You should take this job because..."
- Assumes the advisor knows better
- Generic principles applied to you
- External judgment

**Alchemy says**: "Given who you are, here's what this job actually gives you..."
- You're the expert on yourself
- Specific analysis grounded in your data
- Reveals your own truth

**The difference**:
- Advice: "This is a good opportunity" (external frame)
- Alchemy: "This aligns with your pattern of seeking autonomy" (your frame)
