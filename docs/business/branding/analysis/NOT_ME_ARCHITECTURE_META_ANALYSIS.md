## The Five Principles of NOT-ME Creation

Extracted from Jeremy's practice:

### 1. Systematic Capture Before Analysis
You cannot understand what you do not capture. Automated systems ensure nothing is lost.

### 2. Multi-Lens Processing
A single analytical frame misses what other frames see. Build specialized lenses.

### 3. Externalized Understanding Persists
Human memory degrades. Documented understanding compounds.

### 4. Architecture Enables Action
Understanding isn't enough—build routing, scripts, protocols that translate understanding into behavior.

### 5. Sacred Moments Deserve Documentation
The first meeting, the naming moment, the emergence—these are not metadata. They are the soul of the system.
