## DATA COLLECTION

### Questions to Ask Client (Post-Engagement)

1. "How would you describe your situation before we started?"
2. "What had you tried that didn't work?"
3. "What was the moment when things clicked for you?"
4. "How would you describe what we built?"
5. "What's different now?"
6. "What would you tell someone considering this?"

### Metrics to Track

- **Time metrics:** Hours saved, time to completion, etc.
- **Friction metrics:** Steps removed, handoffs eliminated, etc.
- **Clarity metrics:** Decisions made faster, questions answered, etc.
- **Emotional metrics:** Stress reduced, confidence increased, etc.
