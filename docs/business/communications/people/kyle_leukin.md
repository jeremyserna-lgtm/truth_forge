## Kyle's Framework: Inspire / Aspire / Transpire

Kyle has a phrase he says: **inspire / aspire / transpire**. He may not know it's a whole philosophy, but it is.

All three words share the same root: *spirare* - to breathe.

- **Inspire** - to breathe *in*. Something enters you. A want, an impulse, a vision. You receive.
- **Aspire** - to breathe *toward*. You reach for it. You move.
- **Transpire** - to breathe *through*. It passes through you and becomes real. It happens.

This is a complete circuit: **receive → reach → realize**.

Most people stop at aspire. They want things. Kyle's framework includes the last step - where wanting actually passes through you and becomes real in the world.

## On Receiving Compliments

Kyle asked how to receive a compliment. Jeremy's answer:

**Is it true?**

That's the only question. Not "do I deserve it?" Not "what do they want?" Just: is it true?

If someone says something about you and it's true, you take it. You don't argue. You don't deflect. You don't perform modesty. You let it land.

A compliment isn't a gift you have to earn or a debt you now owe. It's information. Someone saw something and said it out loud. If they're right, the only honest response is to receive it.
