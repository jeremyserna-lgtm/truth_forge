### What the Misinterpretation Reveals

**The observer's framework:**
- Stillness = misery
- Talking = yapping
- Deep conversation = ranting and raving
- Silence = suffering
- Engagement = visible reaction

**The actual framework:**
- Stillness = processing
- Talking = developmental dialogue
- Deep conversation = seeing and being seen
- Silence = integration
- Engagement = internal transformation

**Neither framework is wrong.** They're just looking at different things.

The observer was applying a perfectly reasonable interpretation: when someone looks still and unresponsive while another person talks at length, they're probably bored. That's usually true.

**But it wasn't true here.**

And the observer had no way to know that. No way to see the internal process. No way to understand that what looked like suffering was actually the beginning of growth.
